Every student profile — from basic information to parent contacts — stored securely and accessible in seconds.

Professional Report Cards

Generate, approve, and deliver beautiful, fully white-labelled report cards — to print, via SMS summary, and through the parent portal.

  • 4-stage approval: Teacher → DOS → Head Teacher → Admin
  • Marks, grades, class position, comments, conduct, and attendance
  • One-click PDF generation per student or whole class
  • Automatic SMS summary sent to every parent on release
  • Parents view full report online through the parent portal

Smart Timetable Builder

The DOS builds the school timetable with real-time conflict detection — so no teacher is double-booked and no class has two subjects in the same period.

  • Visual grid-based builder for each class and stream
  • Conflicts highlighted instantly in red with clear explanations
  • Every teacher sees their personalised schedule
  • All staff see the full school timetable
  • Published timetable is locked — only DOS can modify

FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Yes — completely. SchoolMind is a desktop application that stores all school data locally on your computer. You can manage students, record attendance, enter marks, generate report cards, and do everything SchoolMind offers without an internet connection. When you connect to the internet, SchoolMind automatically syncs your data to secure cloud storage for backup.
We send you reminder notifications via in-app banner, WhatsApp, and email starting 14 days before your licence expires. After expiry, SchoolMind enters a locked mode where you can still view your data but cannot add or edit records. You have a 30-day grace period to renew. After 6 months without renewal, cloud-stored data is permanently deleted — though local data on your device remains.
Yes. SchoolMind supports both the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) with 1–3 strand scoring, and the traditional grading system with customisable percentage-to-grade boundaries. Your school configures its own grading scale and assessment structure at setup, so SchoolMind adapts exactly to how your school operates.
School data is protected at every layer. Locally, data is stored in secured application files accessible only through SchoolMind with valid login credentials. In transit, all data is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. At rest on our cloud servers, data is encrypted with AES-256 encryption. All user passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text. A complete audit log tracks every action performed within the system.
We accept payment via Bank Transfer and Mobile Money (MTN MoMo and Airtel Money). After payment, we verify your payment and send your licence key to your WhatsApp or email address, along with activation instructions. The process typically takes less than 24 hours on business days.
SchoolMind's primary desktop application runs on Windows 10 and Windows 11. After subscribing, you also get access to the SchoolMind mobile Progressive Web App (PWA), which works on any modern Android or iOS device through your mobile browser — and can be installed directly to your home screen.
It depends on your subscription tier. Starter allows up to 5 user accounts, Standard allows up to 10, and Premium allows unlimited users. Each user is assigned one of four roles — Administrator, Head Teacher, Director of Studies, or Class Teacher — with role-specific access to data and features.
Yes — you get a full 14-day free trial with complete access to all features, no credit card required. Simply download the SchoolMind installer and you're automatically on a free trial from the moment you install it. At the end of 14 days, you can subscribe to continue — or your account will be deactivated without any charge.
Yes — on the Premium plan. SchoolMind includes a parent portal where parents can log in to view their child's full report card, attendance record, and fee balance. When the Admin releases report cards, an SMS is automatically sent to each parent with a summary and a link to the portal.
All SchoolMind schools get access to in-app help documentation, WhatsApp support (Monday–Friday, 8 AM–6 PM EAT), and email support. Premium subscribers additionally receive remote desktop assistance — where our team can remotely connect to your school computer to resolve technical issues directly. We respond to all support requests within 2 business hours on WhatsApp.
Installation takes under 2 minutes. Initial school setup — entering your school name, logo, classes, and grading configuration — takes about 30 minutes. You can then import your entire student list using our CSV bulk import template, which can process hundreds of students in seconds. Most schools are fully operational on the same day they install SchoolMind.
Absolutely. You can upgrade from Starter to Standard or Standard to Premium at any time by contacting us via WhatsApp or email. We calculate the prorated difference for your remaining subscription period and issue a new licence key for your upgraded tier. All your existing data is preserved during the upgrade.
